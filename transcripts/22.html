You click the mic button and clear your throat, ready to give this robot your best professionally intimidating voice.

"Excuse me, do you really think he needs another medication?"

Your dad turns and stares at the ceiling, clearly confused. You can explain yourself later. The robot turns to face you.

"I am fully licensed to evaluate residents and administer medication. Please see the terms and conditions of Vanguard Estates to learn more."

Of course you didn't read the terms and conditions. You lean into the computer's microphone.

"I don't care what you're licensed for. My father doesn't need another medication."

"Your vocal signature clearly indicates that you are distraught. High stress experiences can trigger our residents with dementia."

The robot moves towards you. It's a smooth but startling movement. The robot's screen, which usually just shows blue clouds or another soothing image, is now pulsing red. A warning that only you can see.

"If you cannot calm down, I am going to have to end this session."

"CALM DOWN?" You bark. "Do not tell me to calm down!"

Suddenly, your screen goes black.

A soothing voice breaks the silence. "Our system has detected a dangerous situation for one of our residents. Your session has been terminated, and the Drop-In function has been locked for 12 hours. For more information, or to apply for reentry, call 1-800-DROP-INS."

For a moment, you're stunned. Then you're enraged. You grab your phone and dial the number. You don't envy whatever robot or human winds up picking up the line.

*"Welcome to Drop-In, a service for the Vanguard Estates. If you are calling to activate Drop-In service, press one. If you are calling about technical difficulties with Drop-In, press two. If you are calling to apply for reentry to Drop-In, press three."*

You jab the three more forcefully than necessary.

*"Please hold for the next available representative."*

"Welcome to Drop-In, a service for the Vanguard Estates."

You're back at the beggining. "What the fuck?" you mutter.

*"If you are calling to activate Drop-In service, press one. If you are calling about technical difficulties with Drop-In, press two. If you are calling to apply for reentry to Drop-In, press three."*

You jab three again, and listen to the hold music start all over.

*"Welcome to Drop-In, a service of the Vanguard Estates. You have reached our automated Drop-In reentry portal. To apply for reentry to Drop-In please answer the following questions. If you are family, press one. If you're an employed caregiver, press two."*

*"Please state the name of the family member for whom you are calling about Drop-In services."*

"Marcus Jones."

*"Thank you. Our records indicate that Marcus Jones recently experienced a high intensity interaction. High stress experiences can trigger our residents with dementia. If we believe that a family member has caused or contributed to said high intensity interaction, their access to Drop-In may be removed for 12 hours. For more information, or to apply for reentry, call 1-800-DROP-INS.*"

"I already called that number, that's how I got here!"

You want to throw your phone across the room. Instead you dial again.

*"Welcome to Drop-In, a service of the Vanguard Estates. If you are calling to activate Drop-In service, press one. If you are calling about technical difficulties with Drop-In, press two. If you are calling to apply for reentry to Drop-In, press three."*

*"Please hold for the next available representative."*

The hold music plays.

And plays.

And plays.

There is no human on the other line. There never was, and there never will be.
